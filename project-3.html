    About This Project:

    This portfolio is designed to provide a clean, modern, and user-friendly interface where visitors can learn about you, your expertise, and your past work. It includes various sections such as About, Experience, Projects, and Contact, ensuring a seamless navigation experience.


    Key Features:

    • ✅ Responsive Design: Works smoothly on all devices, including desktops, tablets, and mobiles.
    • ✅ Theme Customization: Users can switch between different background colors.
    • ✅ Smooth Navigation: A well-structured navbar and a mobile-friendly hamburger menu.
    • ✅ Project Showcase: Displays your recent work with images, descriptions, and live demo links.
    • ✅ Social Media Links: Direct access to your LinkedIn and GitHub profiles.
    • ✅ Contact Section: Allows visitors to reach out via email or LinkedIn.

    Technologies Used:

    • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
    • Bootstrap for styling
